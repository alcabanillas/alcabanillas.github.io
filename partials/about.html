BIO

My first computer was a gift when I was 8 years old and although I did not fully understand how it worked, I began to love the world of programming

Later, in high school, there was an optional subject in Pascal programming and then I decided that I wanted to become a developer so I studied from 1994 to 2000 Computer Engineering at Universitat Autonoma de Barcelona.

My first job was migrating to the 2000 effect an internal application for a construction company that was made in Delphi and since then I made several changes until I arrived to a contact center company and I discovered that the customer experience was something that I was pasionated to.

Since then, my job always was related to the contact section of the companies until I arrived at Enghouse Interactive a contact center software company in 2008.

My first position in the company, was working in the operations team, leading international projects located in EMEA and USA and after some time I moved to the development team where I have been working with the chat module.

Now I have decided to expand my knowledge by coursing an MIT xPro course based on the MERN Stack. You will find some of the projects of the course in my portfolio
